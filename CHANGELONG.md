# Version 2.0.0 (2020-11-18)
- Released a new version of the Spatial Equity Tool with lots of new features
  based on user feedback.

## New features
- New geographic levels of analysis: Users can now upload national, state,
  county, or city level data into the tool and analyze disparities. Users just
  have to choose their appropriate geography at the beginning
- Additional baseline datasets: For the demographic disparities, users can now
  compare against the child and extremely low income populations. For the geographic disparities, users can now compare against the
  senior, child, and extremely low income populations. 
- Additional demographic sub-geography scores: If users upload national or state
  level data to the tool, the demogrpahic disparity chart will also display the
  demographic disparity scores of sub-geographies (states and counties respectively)
